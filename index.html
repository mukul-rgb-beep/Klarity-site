Why It's Different

Not all visualizations
are created equal.

Most disk analyzers use sunburst charts. Research shows rectangular layouts are measurably better at comparing file sizes. Klarity gives you both treemap and icicle views.

◫ Klarity

Treemap

Rectangles sized by file size. Your eye compares areas instantly. No angle math, no distortion at different depths.

▤ Klarity

Icicle / Flame Graph

Horizontal bars aligned on the same baseline. Compare folders at the same level by looking at bar width. Hierarchy is depth, not angle.

◎ Others

Sunburst

Encodes size as arc angle. Outer rings look visually larger even when they're not. Brendan Gregg calls them "the new pie chart."

What's New

Build 1054 — now with
delete power.

Klarity is no longer read-only. Move files to Trash, see scan summaries at a glance, and pick up right where you left off.

New
🗑

Move to Trash

Delete directly from Klarity

Right-click any file or folder in the treemap, icicle chart, or largest items list to move it to Trash. A dedicated Trash button also appears in the info panel. Scan auto-refreshes after deletion.

New
🔒

Slide-to-Confirm Safety

Two-tier delete protection

Large folders (≥1 GB) require a slide-to-unlock gesture, then a confirm button. Smaller items use a simple alert. Prevents accidental mass deletion of important data.

New
✓

Scan Complete Banner

Pulsing summary toast

After every scan, a pulsing green banner shows items scanned, total size, and time elapsed. Auto-dismisses after a few seconds, or tap to close immediately.

Improved
📂

Remember Last Path

Persistent scan location

Klarity remembers which folder you last scanned across app launches. No more re-navigating to the same directory every time you open the app.
